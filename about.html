Our Previous Work

In addition to providing software development and consultancy services to our customers, mCognito Labs carries out in-house research and development of eLearning solutions.

Based on our state of the art Knowledge Retention Estimation Algorithm rooted in cognitive science we are developing applications for effective individual and group learning for both web and mobile platforms.

An early version of this core technology has been successfully used in our pilot mobile applications for learning Japanese Kana alphabets for both Android and iPhone.

We are actively seeking cooperation with educational institutions to obtain a good test base for our future eLearning products.
